  • Squirrels

    An agile tree-dwelling rodent with a bushy tail, typically feeding on nuts and seeds.

  • Rabbits

    A burrowing, gregarious, plant-eating mammal with long ears, long hind legs, and a short tail.

  • Chipmunks

    A burrowing ground squirrel with cheek pouches and light and dark stripes running down the body, found in North America and northern Eurasia.

  • Hawks

    A bird of prey with broad rounded wings and a long tail, typically taking prey by surprise with a short chase.

  • Bird Nests

    The spot in which a bird lays and incubates its eggs and raises its young.

  • Carpenter Bees

    A large solitary bee with purplish wings that nests in tunnels bored in dead wood or plant stems.
